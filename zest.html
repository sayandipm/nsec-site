Welcome to ZEST 2026

The Ultimate Intra-College Sports Championship

ZEST (Zenith of Excellence in Sports Tournament) is our college's premier intra-college general championship, where students compete across various sporting events to earn glory for their respective departments. This tournament celebrates athleticism, sportsmanship, teamwork, and the indomitable spirit of competition.

Championship Format

Each department competes to earn maximum points across all events. Points are awarded based on performance: Gold (10 points), Silver (7 points), Bronze (5 points). The department with the highest cumulative points wins the prestigious ZEST General Championship Trophy!
